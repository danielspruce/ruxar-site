About Ruxar

Ruxar is an Australian independent game studio specialising in precision platformers. Founded in 2014, the studio has released two games on Steam — iZBOT (2015) and iZBOT 2 (2022) — both built around tight controls, instant-death challenge, and bite-sized levels that reward mastery. iZBOT holds a Very Positive rating on Steam with a 9/10 score from 219 reviews.
