FAQ

Common questions.

What is iZBOT 2?

iZBOT 2 is the sequel to iZBOT, a precision platformer by Australian indie studio Ruxar. It features expanded levels, new mechanics, and the same tight controls as the original — but harder. Available on Steam for $9.99.

Do I need to play iZBOT before iZBOT 2?

No — iZBOT 2 is a standalone game. You can jump straight in. That said, playing the original iZBOT first gives you a great foundation for the mechanics and difficulty curve.

What's new in iZBOT 2 compared to the original?

iZBOT 2 adds new mechanics, more levels, and refined level design — doubling down on the precision platformer formula while introducing fresh challenges not in the first game.

Is iZBOT 2 harder than iZBOT?

Yes. iZBOT 2 is harder than the original. If you've mastered iZBOT, the sequel provides a satisfying next challenge. If you're new to the series, starting with the original iZBOT is recommended.
