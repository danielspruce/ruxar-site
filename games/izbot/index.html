FAQ

Common questions.

What is iZBOT?

iZBOT is a fast-paced precision platformer by Australian indie studio Ruxar. You guide a nimble robot through hazard-filled levels with tight controls and instant respawn. Available on Steam for $9.99.

What platforms is iZBOT on?

iZBOT is available on Steam for Windows, macOS, and Linux. Full controller support is included.

How hard is iZBOT?

iZBOT is designed around difficulty and mastery. Levels are short and instantly retryable, so failure is fast and progress is always visible. It's challenging but fair — every death teaches you something.

Is there a sequel to iZBOT?

Yes — iZBOT 2 is available on Steam. It expands on the original with new mechanics, more levels, and the same tight controls — just harder.
