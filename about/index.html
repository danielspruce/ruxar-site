Founder · Developer · Designer

Ruxar is a solo studio. That means one person handled the code, the design, the art direction, the marketing, and everything in between for both games.

iZBOT started as a personal project and shipped on Steam in 2015. iZBOT 2 followed in 2022. The postmortem for iZBOT was published in Game Developer Magazine.

PHILOSOPHY

What we believe.

⚡

Instant Feedback

Every death tells you exactly what went wrong. No ambiguity, no excuses. You failed that jump. Try again.

🎯

Mastery Over Luck

Every level is solvable. The difficulty is real, not random. When you beat a hard section, you earned it.

⏱

Respect Your Time

Bite-sized levels. Instant respawns. No unskippable cutscenes. Your time is spent playing, not waiting.
