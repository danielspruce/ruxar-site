What Is a Photo Mosaic?

A photo mosaic is a large image made up of dozens — or thousands — of smaller photos arranged in a grid. From a distance you see the main portrait or scene; up close, every tile is its own distinct photograph. It's one of the most personal gifts you can give, and Photomosaica lets you create one entirely on your own computer.
